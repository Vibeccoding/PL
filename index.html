About Pacific Life

Company Overview

Pacific Life provides a variety of products and services designed to help individuals and businesses in the retail, institutional, workforce benefits, and reinsurance markets achieve financial security. Founded in 1868 and headquartered in Newport Beach, California, Pacific Life is a leading provider of life insurance, annuities, and asset management services serving individuals, businesses, and institutions with a diverse portfolio of financial products.

Modernization & Digital Transformation

Pacific Life has undertaken significant modernization efforts including unified technology platforms, enhanced customer experience through personalized digital services, and upgraded administration data systems processing over 27 million records. Our product innovation focuses on indexed universal life insurance and registered index-linked annuity products, providing flexible solutions tailored to customer protection and retirement needs.

Global Expansion

Operations in 23 countries with strategic expansion in Japan, Netherlands, UK, and Canada through Pacific Life Re.

Pacific Life History Timeline

1868

Pioneering Foundation

January 2, 1868: The Pacific Mutual Life Insurance Company of California was certified for business and founded in Sacramento, beginning our legacy of financial protection.

1881

San Francisco Expansion

Pacific Mutual moved its headquarters to San Francisco, where investment opportunities abounded. By the start of the 20th century, Pacific Mutual had become a national company with agents operating in 40 states and territories.

1885

Accident Department Launch

March 32, 1885: Pacific Mutual committee voted to organize an accident department for its life insurance company, beginning to offer accident policies to protect policyholders from unexpected loss of income.

1905

National Presence Achieved

Pacific Life reaches $61 million of life insurance in force with a presence in 40 states and territories, establishing itself as a major national insurance company.

1906

Los Angeles Headquarters

After the home office was destroyed in an earthquake, Pacific Life headquarters moved to Los Angeles, marking a new chapter in the company's growth.

1914

Agent Training Innovation

The company established a correspondence program, the School for Salesmen, to offer better training to its agents, pioneering professional development in the industry.

1932

Community Support Begins

Pacific Life begins supporting the United Way, marking the start of our long-standing commitment to community philanthropy and social responsibility.

1941

Group Annuities Launch

Pacific Life issues its first group annuity pension contract, expanding into retirement solutions and laying the foundation for our annuities business.

1955

Technology Pioneer

Pacific Life installs the first UNIVAC computer in a private company west of Mississippi, demonstrating early adoption of technology for business operations.

1972

Newport Beach Move

The home office moves to its current location in Newport Beach, establishing the headquarters that continues to serve as our primary base of operations today.

1983

Distribution Model Evolution

Pacific Life starts a relationship with the M Group and begins transitioning from a career agency model to an independent brokerage model, revolutionizing our distribution strategy.

1984

Foundation Established

The Pacific Mutual Foundation is established with a $3 million endowment, formalizing our commitment to philanthropic giving and community impact.

1997

Mutual Holding Company

Pacific Life converts to a mutual holding company to increase financial flexibility, enabling greater strategic opportunities and business expansion.

2001

Mutual Funds Launch

Pacific Life introduces its first line of retail mutual funds, expanding our investment offerings and providing more comprehensive financial solutions to customers.

2005

Nebraska Domicile & Expansion

The company moves its state of domicile to Nebraska and opens the Omaha operations center. Aviation Capital Group becomes a wholly owned subsidiary, diversifying our business portfolio.

2008

Global Expansion

Pacific Life purchases Scottish Re Group Limited and creates the subsidiary Pacific Life Re, significantly expanding our international reinsurance capabilities.

2016

Virginia Operations

Pacific Life purchases a new term life insurance platform and launches a business center in Lynchburg, Virginia, enhancing our operational capacity and geographic presence.

2018

150 Years Milestone

Pacific Life celebrates 150 years of supporting financial advisors and policyholders, marking a century and a half of continuous service and innovation in financial services.

2025

Global Modernization

Pacific Life completes major digital transformation with unified technology platforms processing 27+ million records, launches innovative indexed universal life and registered index-linked annuity products, and expands global operations to 23 countries.
