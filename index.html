About Pacific Life

Company Overview

Pacific Life provides a variety of products and services designed to help individuals and businesses in the retail, institutional, workforce benefits, and reinsurance markets achieve financial security. Whether your goal is to protect loved ones or grow your assets for retirement, Pacific Life offers innovative life insurance and annuity solutions, as well as mutual funds, that provide value and financial security for current and future generations.

Founded in 1868 and headquartered in Newport Beach, California, Pacific Life is one of the largest life insurance companies in the U.S., serving millions of customers across the nation. As a member of the FORTUNE 500, we are recognized for financial strength, innovation, and customer service excellence.

Core Business & Services

Our comprehensive portfolio includes Life Insurance (Term, Whole, Universal), Annuities (Fixed, Indexed, Variable), and Investment Solutions (Asset Management, Pension Risk Transfer, Structured Settlements). We serve individuals, financial professionals, and businesses with tailored financial strategies designed to meet diverse needs and goals.

Modernization & Digital Transformation

Pacific Life has undertaken significant modernization efforts including unified technology platforms, enhanced customer experience through personalized digital services, and upgraded administration data systems processing over 27 million records. Our product innovation focuses on new indexed universal life insurance and registered index-linked annuity products, providing flexible solutions tailored to customer protection and retirement needs.

Global Reach & Market Position

While primarily U.S.-focused, Pacific Life has a growing international presence through investment management and reinsurance operations. We now operate in 23 countries through subsidiaries and strategic partnerships across North America, Europe, and Asia, with recent Savings & Retirement transactions in Japan, the Netherlands, the UK, and Canada.

Pacific Life History Timeline

1868

Pioneering Foundation

January 2, 1868: The Pacific Mutual Life Insurance Company of California was certified for business and founded in Sacramento, beginning our legacy of financial protection.

1881

San Francisco Expansion

Pacific Mutual moved its headquarters to San Francisco, where investment opportunities abounded. By the start of the 20th century, Pacific Mutual had become a national company with agents operating in 40 states and territories.

1885

Accident Department Launch

March 32, 1885: Pacific Mutual committee voted to organize an accident department for its life insurance company, beginning to offer accident policies to protect policyholders from unexpected loss of income.

1905

National Presence Achieved

Pacific Life reaches $61 million of life insurance in force with a presence in 40 states and territories, establishing itself as a major national insurance company.

1906

Los Angeles Headquarters

After the home office was destroyed in an earthquake, Pacific Life headquarters moved to Los Angeles, marking a new chapter in the company's growth and resilience.

1914

Agent Training Innovation

The company established a correspondence program, the School for Salesmen, to offer better training to its agents, pioneering professional development in the industry.

1932

Community Support Begins

Pacific Life begins supporting the United Way, marking the start of our long-standing commitment to community philanthropy and social responsibility.

1936

Financial Resilience

Popular non-cancellable disability policies threatened Pacific Life's financial security. California's insurance commissioner ruled that the company must be reorganized, demonstrating adaptability and regulatory compliance.

1941

Group Annuities Launch

Pacific Life issues its first group annuity pension contract, expanding into retirement solutions and laying the foundation for our annuities business.

1955

Technology Pioneer

Pacific Life installs the first UNIVAC computer in a private company west of Mississippi, demonstrating early adoption of technology for business operations.

1972

Newport Beach Move

The home office moves to its current location in Newport Beach, establishing the headquarters that continues to serve as our primary base of operations today.

1983

Distribution Model Evolution

Pacific Life starts a relationship with the M Group and begins transitioning from a career agency model to an independent brokerage model, revolutionizing our distribution strategy.

1984

Foundation Established

The Pacific Mutual Foundation is established with a $3 million endowment, formalizing our commitment to philanthropic giving and community impact.

1992

Industry Leadership

The company wins the right to rehabilitate the failing First Capital Life and provide protection for its former policyholders, demonstrating commitment to industry stability.

1994

PIMCO Spin-off

PIMCO, the successful investment management subsidiary of Pacific Life, is spun off into an independent company, allowing both entities to focus on their core strengths.

1997

Mutual Holding Company

Pacific Life converts to a mutual holding company to increase financial flexibility, enabling greater strategic opportunities and business expansion.

2001

Mutual Funds Launch

Pacific Life introduces its first line of retail mutual funds, expanding our investment offerings and providing more comprehensive financial solutions to customers.

2005

Nebraska Domicile & Strategic Expansion

The company moves its state of domicile to Nebraska, saving millions in taxes, and opens the Omaha operations center. Aviation Capital Group becomes a wholly owned subsidiary, diversifying our business portfolio.

2008

Global Expansion

Pacific Life purchases Scottish Re Group Limited and creates the subsidiary Pacific Life Re, significantly expanding our international reinsurance capabilities and global footprint.

2010

Retirement Solutions Division

The Retirement Solutions Division is created from the Annuities and Mutual Funds Division to offer broader solutions to income needs and retirement planning.

2016

Virginia Operations

Pacific Life purchases a new term life insurance platform and launches a business center in Lynchburg, Virginia, enhancing our operational capacity and geographic presence.

2018

150 Years Milestone

Pacific Life celebrates 150 years of supporting financial advisors and policyholders, marking a century and a half of continuous service and innovation in financial services.

2025

Global Modernization

Pacific Life completes major digital transformation with unified technology platforms processing 27+ million records, launches innovative indexed universal life and registered index-linked annuity products, and expands global operations to 23 countries.
